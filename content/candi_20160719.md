Title: CREATIVE PROBLEM SOLVING LED TO MILLIONS IN SAVINGS
Date: 2016-07-19 10:00
Category: 家長, 教師
Tags: children, CS, future, innovation, problem-solving, programming, tech skill
Author: CandiRai

A fortune 500 insurance company proves that improving processes not only saves money in the long term, but also noticeably increases associate engagements. The company started implementing Lean principles in its Application Maintenance area three years ago. The transformation involved more than 2,000 IT engineers, requiring associates across all IT organizations to collaborate to identify opportunities for improvement, map out changes, implement them and make plans for sustainability of the changes. The transformation saves company $40 million annually. Beyond saving time and money, Lean challenges associates to use their skills to their full potential.  The associates are much happier at work and more productive.
